London

Why Visit London?

Nestled around the Thames River, London is a sprawling metropolis with every attraction imaginable. Eat at over 6,000 restaurants, or shop at one of the largest malls in the world, Selfridges. Visit the royal palace, or watch a Shakespheare play live on stage. If the hustle and bustle becomes a bit much, relax in one of London’s many public parks.
